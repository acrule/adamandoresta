Exploring Eugene

Eugene is Track Town USA, home to the University of Oregon, and situated in the lush Willamette River Valley. Some of our favorite activities around town including biking along the Willamette, hiking Spencer Butte, visiting the King Estate Winery, and enjoying Voodoo Donuts.

Many of the 7 Wonders of Oregon including Mt Hood, Crater Lake, the Oregon Coast, and the Columbia River Gorge are just a few hours drive away. Portland, Seattle, Vancouver, and Vancouver Island are all just a short flight away. Why not come for the wedding and stay for the Northwest?

We are currently looking into reserving a block of rooms at a hotel in Eugene for Saturday and Sunday nights (6/3 - 6/5). Stay tuned! In the meantime, you might try Airbnb or VRBO.
