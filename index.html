About Adam by Oresta

When he’s not hacking away or thinking really hard, he enjoys climbing, surfing, and ultra-lightweight backpacking. Exploring Eugene

Eugene is Track Town USA, home to the University of Oregon, and situated in the lush Willamette River Valley. Some of our favorite activities around town including biking along the Willamette, hiking Spencer's Butte, visiting the King Estate Winery, and enjoying Voodoo Donuts.

Many of the 7 Wonders of Oregon including Mt Hood, Crater Lake, the Oregon Coast, and the Columbia River Gorge are just a few hours drive away. Portland, Seattle, Vancouver, and Vancouver Island are all just a short flight away. Why not come for the wedding and stay for the Northwest?
