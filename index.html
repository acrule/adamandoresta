About Adam by Oresta

When he’s not hacking away or thinking really hard, he enjoys climbing, surfing, and ultra-lightweight backpacking. He also has a passion for donuts and can be frequently spotted in his natural habitat—Rose Donuts—savoring a buttermilk bar.

Visiting Eugene

Eugene is Track Town USA, home to the University of Oregon, and situated in the lush Willamette River Valley. Some of our favorite activities around town including biking along the Willamette, hiking Spencer Butte, visiting the King Estate Winery, and enjoying Voodoo Donuts.

Many of the 7 Wonders of Oregon including Mt Hood, Crater Lake, the Oregon Coast, and the Columbia River Gorge are just a few hours drive away. Portland, Seattle, Vancouver, and Vancouver Island are all just a short flight away. Why not come for the wedding and stay for the Northwest?

We have held a block of rooms at the Comfort Suites in Eugene, just around the corner from Mt Pisgah. Call (541) 343-7000 and ask for the "Tolmach / Rule Wedding" to get a rate of $129/$138 for a King/Double Queen room for Saturday and Sunday nights (6/3 - 6/5). We also suggest you try the Holiday Inn and Comfort Suites in nearby Springfield (~15 minutes from Mt Pisgah) and ask for the wedding rate. There are also many homes for rent on Airbnb or VRBO.
